###############################################################################
## RF2 Community Patch (SOPOT)
###############################################################################

RF2 Community Patch (SOPOT) is a compatibility and enhancement patch project
for Red Faction II.

This file documents third-party and upstream licensing/attribution items that
are implemented in this repository.

---------------------------------
Upstream lineage
---------------------------------

SOPOT is structured after Alpine Faction and includes code/structure derived
from both Alpine Faction and Dash Faction.

Alpine Faction homepage:
https://github.com/GooberRF/alpinefaction

Dash Faction homepage:
https://github.com/rafalh/dashfaction

Alpine Faction and Dash Faction source code are licensed under the Mozilla
Public License 2.0.

MPL 2.0 reference:
https://www.mozilla.org/MPL/2.0/

---------------------------------
Third-party and upstream components currently implemented in SOPOT
---------------------------------

- subhook (vendor/subhook)
- Direct3D8 headers (vendor/d3d8)
- d3d8to9 (vendor/d3d8to9)
